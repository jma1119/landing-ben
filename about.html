ABOUT US

Ben’s Halo-Halo, is a beloved Laguna brand, has expanded further into Cavite, bringing it's signature halo-halo hits to the south. The first Ben’s Halo-Halo opened in San Pablo, Laguna in the year of 2013. crossing over to Trece Martires, tanza and Imus in neighboring Cavite. This latest branch occupies a small, unassuming space near Silang, Cavite’s town plaza.
