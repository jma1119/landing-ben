BEN'S HALO-HALO

Ben’s Halo-Halo comes in many variants: Regular, Spicy Winter,Salty Summer, Macapuno con yelo, Saba con yelo and Mais con yelo. Spicy Winter adds bite through a generous sprinkling of chili powder, with a whole siling labuyo as the proverbial cherry on top. meanwhile Salty Summer, tops the sweet melange of the traditional halo-halo with half a salted egg. Each glass of Ben’s halo-halo carries three layers. Minatamis na saging, mashed white beans, and macapuno form the base, while more colorful scoops of ube, leche flan, and sweetened langka sit atop each glass.
